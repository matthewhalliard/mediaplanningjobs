## About the role

Horizon Media is hiring a Senior Media Planner to lead cross-channel campaigns for a portfolio of CPG and DTC brands. You'll own the planning lifecycle end-to-end — RFP response, audience strategy, channel mix, reach & frequency modeling, flowcharting, and post-campaign reporting.

## Responsibilities

- Develop integrated media plans across linear TV, CTV, paid social, programmatic display, audio, and OOH
- Build and present recommendations to clients with clear strategic rationale
- Partner with buying, analytics, and strategy teams to translate plans into executions
- Mentor junior planners and assistants

## Requirements

- 4–6 years agency-side media planning experience
- Strong fluency with Nielsen, Comscore, MRI-Simmons, and standard planning tools
- Track record on accounts > $5M annual media spend
- Excellent client-facing communication
