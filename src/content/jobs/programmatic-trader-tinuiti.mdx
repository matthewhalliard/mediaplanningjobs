## About the role

Tinuiti is hiring a Programmatic Trader focused on Connected TV. You'll run hands-on-keyboard campaign execution across The Trade Desk, DV360, and Amazon DSP for performance-driven CTV programs.

## Responsibilities

- Set up, launch, and optimize CTV programmatic campaigns across major DSPs
- Manage daily pacing, bid strategy, and inventory mix
- Build private marketplace deals with publishers and SSPs
- Partner with analytics on incrementality and lift testing

## Requirements

- 2–4 years of hands-on DSP experience (TTD, DV360, or Amazon DSP)
- Strong understanding of CTV inventory, deal types, and frequency management
- Comfortable with Excel/Sheets pivot tables; SQL is a plus
