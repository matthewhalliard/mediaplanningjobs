## About the role

Publicis Media is hiring a Media Analyst to join the marketing mix modeling practice. You'll partner with planning teams to translate MMM outputs into actionable budget recommendations.

## Responsibilities

- Run and interpret MMM analyses for blue-chip CPG and retail clients
- Build dashboards in Tableau or Looker for ongoing performance reads
- Translate model outputs into clear recommendations for media planners
- Support incrementality and lift test design

## Requirements

- 2–4 years in marketing analytics, with MMM exposure
- Strong SQL and Python or R
- Ability to communicate technical work to non-technical planners
