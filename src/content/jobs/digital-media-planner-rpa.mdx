## About the role

RPA is hiring a Digital Media Planner to support automotive and financial services accounts. You'll build digital media plans across display, video, social, and search.

## Responsibilities

- Develop digital media recommendations and tactical plans
- Manage RFP processes with publisher partners
- Build and maintain media flowcharts and pacing reports
- Partner with buying and ad ops on execution

## Requirements

- 2–3 years agency planning experience, digital-led
- Familiarity with audience tools (Comscore, MRI, Resonate)
- Strong Excel/Sheets and PowerPoint
