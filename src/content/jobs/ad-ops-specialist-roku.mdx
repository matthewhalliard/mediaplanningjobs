## About the role

Roku is hiring an Ad Operations Specialist to traffic and QA campaigns on Roku's CTV ad platform. You'll work directly with sales and planning teams to launch advertiser campaigns flawlessly.

## Responsibilities

- Traffic, schedule, and QA video campaigns on Roku's ad server
- Troubleshoot creative and tag issues
- Partner with sales on pre-sale technical scoping
- Pull and deliver client reports

## Requirements

- 1–2 years digital ad ops experience (CTV/OTT preferred)
- Familiarity with VAST, ad servers, and creative QA tools
- Detail-oriented, comfortable with high-volume execution
