## About the role

Spotify is hiring an Ad Operations Manager to lead campaign trafficking, QA, and yield workflows for our direct-sold audio and video advertising business. You'll manage a team of 3–4 ad ops specialists and partner closely with sales, planning, and engineering.

## Responsibilities

- Own end-to-end campaign execution from IO to reporting
- Manage and develop a team of ad operations specialists
- Partner with product on ad server (GAM) and creative tooling improvements
- Drive process automation and reduce manual trafficking time

## Requirements

- 5+ years in digital ad operations with at least 2 in a leadership role
- Deep knowledge of GAM, VAST/VPAID, and IAB standards
- Experience with audio or video ad formats strongly preferred
